INGREDIENTS

  • 1 table spoon of tea powder
  • Required amount of Milk (3/4 cup of milk)
  • Required amount of sugar(11/2of sugar)

METHOD/PROCEDURE TO MAKE TEA

  1. To make chai,Add tea powder into a bowl
  2. Add sugar
  3. Add 1/2 cup of water
  4. After 2-3 min of boiling,You can add Milk
  5. Boil all the ingredients together on a medium flame
  6. Strain immediately using strainer and discard the remains of tea powder
  7. Now Serve The Tea

Hot and Delicious Tea is Ready!!!!
