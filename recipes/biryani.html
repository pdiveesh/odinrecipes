CHICKEN DUM BIRYANI


DumBiryani,a classic dish of the Mughal Nizams is an eye catching aromatic rice loved by all.

INGREDIENTS

FOR RICE

  • 2 cups of basmati rice
  • 2 to 21/2litres of water
  • 1 tsp shahi jeera
  • 1 bay leaf(small)
  • 2 inch cinnamon (thin)
  • 3 green cardamoms
  • 4 cloves(optional)
