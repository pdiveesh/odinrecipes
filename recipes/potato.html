Potato Fry


Potato Fry is a spicy, tasty side dish made by cooking potatoes with spices, onion & curry leaves.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 heaped cup potato chopped
  • Required amount of salt
  • 1 tbsp oil(gingelly oil)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp jeera
  • few curry leaves
  • 1 no garlic crushed
  • a tiny pinch hing
  • 1/8 tsp of Turmeric powder
  • 3/4 tsp of chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp of sambarpowder

Procedure

  1. Cut potato into small bite sized cubes, comes to 1 heaped cup. Rinse well, drain water and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a kadai - add mustard seeds let it splutter then add jeera, add crushed garlic. Give a quick saute.
  3. Add potato and saute for 2 mins. Then add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt and sambar powder
  4. Saute for 1 minute. Then cook covered for 3-5 mins.
  5. Check if its cooked, turns soft then its perfectly cooked. Keep stirring in low medium flame. Saute for few more minutes till it has crispy edges.
  6. Serve Potato Fry hot with rice and curd perfect combo!
